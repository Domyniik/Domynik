SUSTAINABLE FOOTWEAR PRODUCTION

The world is changing and so is the way we make shoes! Dive deep into the art of sustainable footwear production — where innovation, craftsmanship, and eco-conscious choices come together to shape the future of the industry.

Get a behind-the-scenes look at how Portugal is leading the way in creating high-quality and stylish shoes. From revolutionary materials like vegan leather, recycled fabrics, and plant-based alternatives to ethical manufacturing processes that reduce waste and energy consumption, we’re covering it all!

Whether you're a sneakerhead, eco-conscious consumer, or an aspiring footwear entrepreneur, this series will inspire you to take a step toward a more sustainable future — one shoe at a time.

IN HER SHOES: FOOTWEAR ENTREPRENEURS SHARE THEIR JOURNEY

Ever wondered what it takes to launch a successful shoe brand ? In this inspiring video series, we sit down with female footwear entrepreneurs who turned their passion into thriving businesses. From the first sketch to the final product, they share the highs, the lows, and everything in between.

Get an exclusive behind-the-scenes look at their creative process, the challenges of building a brand, and the lessons they’ve learned along the way. Whether you’re dreaming of launching your own collection or just love a good success story, this series is packed with insights, inspiration, and real talk about making it in the footwear industry.


THE REAL DEAL OF LAUNCHING YOUR FOOTWEAR BRAND

Starting a shoe brand? It’s exciting, but let’s be real—it’s not all glam and easy wins. This series breaks down the real challenges, costs, and decisions behind launching a successful footwear brand. From design and manufacturing to branding and sales, get the insider insights no one talks about.
